About Me

Hello there! This is me. For the sake of this site, you may refer to me as Zyla or just N. I didn't realize it earlier, but I grow up loving to pour what I have in mind or heart through writing. So I guess, it's time to take it further via this site. Enjoy!

Who am I?

Just like you are, I'm an ordinary human who wants to grow more by learning. Coincidentally, I find writing to be a peaceful way to do so.

Physics and Me

I am also a physics student who loves being one. To me, physics or science in general is fascinating when we get a grasp of it. Through this site, I'd love to share some short-writing related to everyday science.

About My Writing

My pieces of writing is generally random and personal. Besides science related wonders, I mainly write about my memories, thoughts, and imaginations--which I still hope can be meaningful in some ways.
