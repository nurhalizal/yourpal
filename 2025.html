A Glimpse of 2025

A page dedicated to store fragments of memories which have shaped the year.

The start of the year was also the beginning of new journeys.

Not only beginnings, I decided to end something: my connection to disturbing things.

There are always uncountable things to be grateful for.
Every breath, every step, every smile, basically everything.
This year, I was also introduced to several new things to learn
Hours of my time were spent being on the way.
Anyway, here's some related (or not related) photos.
Our happiness is in our hands.
There was a moment, when I really needed to tell my self this,
and mind you, I’m gonna continue reminding this vulnerable self about it.
From baking to simple stitches, it’s never too late to let the 'artist' in us to grow, right?
What about taking on a new responsibility?
Yes, I did assign to something before the new term started.
The next days afterwards were busy. At times, it felt hectic.
During those long days, each late afternoon was the perfect moment to 'pause'.
No need to do anything.
Just there, existing, waiting, taking everything all in with a big smile of gratitude and hope.
Staying in a lane of a journey I started before the year started—and had carried the dream of, since years ago.
Until… I made it—to the 'stage' that I thought was the end goal.
But it wasn’t.
The endeavors continued (and still does, doesn’t it?)
More steps were taken, more things were learnt, and even tears were shed.
Here's some more of randomly taken pictures while I was in another city for the event.
Oh yeah, back to the 'story'.
The result?
Thank God, it’s beyond what I could’ve imagined back then.
Before the end of the year, some simple 'getaway' plans with family and friends were realized.
Highlight of this year wasn't a single moment.
Instead, it was a whole long journey.
So, persisting was a big part of it.
