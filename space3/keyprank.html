The Key Prank

May 31, 2025 Random

Apparently, it’s another day of writing things related to school. That’s fair enough cause I’ve been spending most of my years as a student by now. Anyway, let’s see what I’ve got to share this time.

At some point during middle school, I became known as an early bird. I used to be one of the earliest students to arrive. That meant that I’d often find the classroom still locked as I arrived.

Long story short, we bought a new lock for our classroom. Since I normally arrived early, I was given one of the keys, so that I was in charge of locking the door before we left, and opening it as I arrived. To be honest, I enjoyed being the key holder. I wasn’t the only one who had the key, but as I remember, I was often the one who unlocked it in the morning. It was fun arriving when the classroom was still quite then watching it gradually fill up. It felt like whatever happened in class, I was there too. I missed nothing!

It became my daily thing for a while. Everything went pretty normal about being the key holder. But one day, some friends and I decided to have some fun. I opened the door and entered as usual, then an idea popped up of one of my friends who came early that day. We decided to close the door again—as if it had never been opened that morning. We even asked someone out there to help ‘locking’ the door from outside.

One by one of my classmates came, waited in front of the class. They just instantly thought that I and my friend who had the key as well hadn’t arrived yet. Meanwhile, on the other side of the walls, some friends and I were trying to hold our laughter. We tried to make as less noise as possible to not get caught. One of the funniest parts was listening to them chat after some time of waiting. They knew it was unusual for both the other key master and I to come that late. So, they started wondering and guessing what’s going on. I even remember someone said something like this, “What if both of them (who have the keys) can’t make it to school today? Then, we’ll stay like this.” Those words were the mark that our little prank was successful, haha.

We kept it up for a while until quite a lot of my friends had come and waited outside. Then, we finally decided to open the door and show them their surprise. I sadly forgot how they all reacted, but the ‘fun’ of being involved in a little prank remains solid in my memory. Thanks to that little key with the little prank, I got a fun memory to tell and still makes me smile to this day.
