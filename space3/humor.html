My Sense of Humor

March 7, 2025 Random

I am not sure about what it means to have a great sense of humor. But if that means one has the ability to appreciate jokes or easily find things amusing—then yes, I do have a great sense of humor. Yet I still think humor is a complicated abstract noun. Perhaps, there are numerous types of humor and I’m sure enough that not all of that I can find amusing. I guess I’ve seen/watched things that were supposed to be funny but I can’t even smile at it simply because I don’t relate with the type of humor.

However, if we talk about basic humor, or at least the everyday type we find in everyday interaction and communication, I’m kind of way too easy to be amused. I’m also convinced that I can understand jokes easily at times! Sorry, if that sounded too confident, hehe. So, basically, I easily laugh and even sometimes way too easy. I also have big laughter that I’ve been scolded for laughing lots of times or at least being told to lower my voice down when laughing in public spaces. Various types of things can make me laugh. Small jokes said by my friends, unintended funny things, memes, and many more. Not just that, sometimes I almost burst into laughters due to eavesdropping others’ conversation, haha. And it’s always been difficlut for me to hold my laughter. One time, I almost choked on my food or drink because someone suddenly made a joke while I’m swallowing things. So yeah, I basically laugh very easily, and I love laughters! I even have the believe that there’ll always be things to laugh at every single day.

Oh yeah, before closing this piece, I almost forgot. As extra explanation to illustrate my sense of humor, here’s two more things to describe my sense of humor. First, I would likely burst into laughters just by remembering funny things that has happened before even if the moment has long gone. Besides, I sometimes laugh or at least giggle just because others around me are laughing at things that I don’t even understand. Every now and then, I do think of this behaviour of mine as both reasonable and quirky at the same time.

But regardless, having this sense of humor is one of my ways of enjoying every moment.
