My Signature's History

March 13, 2025 Random

I’m not sure how long did it take, but I certainly know that I’ve had the same signature for a long time. It has not always been exactly the same. There are few differences between my current signature compared to the first version of it. My signature is a simple one. Not to mention it looks childish even to me sometimes. But there’s no wonder why a signature looks childish if indeed it’s owned by a child, haha. Based on my memory, I came up with my signature in the middle of elementary school, probably around the age of 9 to 11.

If anyone take a look at my old notebooks, surely, you’ll find some signatures around, here and there. Then if compared to my current one, they wouldn’t look that distinct. The signature is just my initial with some added curves. It has always been like that ever since I was still an elementary school student. I only made a little change by reducing some curves. Back then, I thought that kind of change would make it look less childish. And now, I actually think that modification worked well. Besides making it seem less childish (at least to me), it also has made it easier for me to write my signature.

To this day, I’ve used that signature for several things, my citizenship id, school stuffs, or just scribbling on pieces of papers. Up till now, I still enjoy scribbling my signature on any piece of paper, just as much as I liked to do that back then as a little kid. I have it in mind that my signature is a part of my identity, just like my name.
